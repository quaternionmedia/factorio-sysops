# Factorio: Sysadmin - Technology Tree (Revised)

## Research Structure

IT technologies use a mix of:
- **Vanilla science packs** (integration with main progression)
- **IT science packs** (IT-specific research)

This ensures IT progression is tied to overall factory advancement.

## Integration with Vanilla Research

IT research does NOT replace or modify vanilla research. Players complete vanilla research as normal while optionally pursuing IT research in parallel.

### Synergies

| Vanilla Research | IT Bonus When Combined |
|------------------|------------------------|
| Automation 3 | +10% processing speed with IT monitoring |
| Logistics 3 | +15% robot efficiency with IT coordination |
| Mining Productivity | +5% additional with IT optimization |
| Inserter Capacity | +20% throughput with IT control |

### No Conflicts

- IT research uses different lab configuration
- IT science packs made from different ingredients
- Both trees can be researched simultaneously
- Neither blocks the other
